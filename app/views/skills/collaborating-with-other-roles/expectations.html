Expectations at associate level

  • understand the different roles and responsibilities in your team
  • work closely with the other designers and researchers on your team
  • understand content design and research best practice
  • show designs early and regularly
  • aim to ‘Be agile’ rather than just ‘Do agile’, and understand the difference
  • do not be afraid to ask for help and know who to ask
  • know the types of work you should be doing in each phase of a project

Expectations at working level

  • create opportunities to collaborate with all areas of your team (User centered design, delivery, software development, etc.)
  • understand where the boundaries are between roles and make any crossover an opportunity
  • break down barriers to help the team work more effectively together
  • have confidence to bring a design mindset to the team
  • help teams improve their agile working practices through honest retrospectives
  • help the other roles in your team understand the value of design and what your role involves

Expectations at senior level

  • collaborate at a strategic level with senior stakeholders to get the best possible outcomes for users and DWP
  • encourage good working practices in teams
  • help people to understand each other’s roles
  • act as a mediator and be able to resolve conflict
