Expectations at associate level

  • share your work with other designers and the wider digital community
  • seek out regular feedback from other designers
  • look at how other designers have tackled similar problems
  • attend local and national community events
  • create opportunities to collaborate with other designers
  • give constructive feedback and take onboard feedback given to you
  • be inquisitive. look for opportunities to learn both internally and externally

Expectations at working level

  • support the creation and iteration of common patterns in your work
  • take a lead role in the local design community
  • take an active role in the cross-government design community
  • spot opportunities for others to collaborate, based on your understanding of their work
