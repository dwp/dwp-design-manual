The Principles and guidelines we follow

We follow the GOV.UK Government Design Principles, Service Standard and content design guidance. We also follow the GOV.UK accessibility strategy to improve the accessibility of our services.

We also have our own principles for Designing for agent facing systems in DWP {#

We have published more information where you can find out more about how we do user centred design at DWP.
