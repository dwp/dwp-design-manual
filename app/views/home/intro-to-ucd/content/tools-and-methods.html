Tools and methods used in UCD

Several tools and methods are used in the UCD process to ensure that services are designed effectively:

  • Empathy walls - visual tools to collect and analyse user research findings, displaying insights about users' behaviours, needs, pain points, and opportunities for improvement
  • Journey maps - map out the user's relationship with a service over time, highlighting all touch points and emotional states
  • Card sorting - used to understand how users think about and categorise information
  • Service maps - visual representations of the systems, technologies, data, and agencies involved in a service
  • User stories - define user requirements in concise statements
  • Prototyping and Usability testing - creating mock ups of designs and testing them with real users
