The importance of ongoing user research

User research is critical in the UCD process. It forms the foundation of any successful design by ensuring decisions are based on evidence rather than assumptions or opinions.

This needs to be an ongoing process that occurs throughout a project, not just at the beginning. Needs can change over time, as can the way users interact with a service. Therefore, research should be integrated into every stage of development to iteratively test and refine services based on real user feedback.
