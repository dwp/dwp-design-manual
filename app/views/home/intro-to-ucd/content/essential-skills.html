Essential skills and behaviours for UCD teams

Successfully implementing UCD requires teams to have specific skills and behaviours including:

  • strong empathy to genuinely understand user needs
  • curiosity to explore new ideas
  • creativity to generate diverse solutions
  • collaboration skills for working with cross-functional teams
  • clear communication abilities with users and stakeholders
  • analytical and organisational skills
  • resilience and adaptability
  • commitment to continuous learning

These skills are essential for teams to effectively implement UCD and deliver successful public services.
