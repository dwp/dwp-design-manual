Interaction and Service Design in DWP

Interaction Designers create whole user journeys and individual interactions, making sure products and services are accessible and can be used by everyone who needs them.

Service designers work with teams to realign products, business processes and organisational structures across end-to-end services, they help make sure users can achieve their goals efficiently.
